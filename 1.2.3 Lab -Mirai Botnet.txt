Mirai Botnet Lab_1.2.3
The Mirai botnet is a well-known malware that targets Internet of Things (IoT) devices, turning them into a network of remotely controlled bots used to launch large-scale Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks.

a. What is the vulnerability? 
Usage of Default and Weak Credentials.

b. Who might exploit it? Explain.
Cybercriminals
Hacktivist
Script-Kiddies

c. Why does the vulnerability exist?
Lack of regulation 
Poor Security Practices
User Negligence

d. What could be done to limit the vulnerability?
Firmware update
Change of Default Credentials
Disabling Unused features
Industry Standards
